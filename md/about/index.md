## What I Do

I help organizations translate business strategy into valuable IT solutions. As an Enterprise Architect, I ensure the realization of clients' dreams according to best practices and good principles—acting as a partner throughout the entire process, not just delivering documents and disappearing.

### Core Services

- **Enterprise Architecture**: Strategic IT alignment, governance frameworks, large-scale transformation guidance
- **Software Architecture**: Solution design, integration architecture, API design
- **Team Leadership**: Coaching development teams, establishing best practices, bridging business and technical stakeholders
- **Embedded Systems & IoT**: Hardware prototyping, embedded software development, wireless sensor networks

"Passion makes the world go round." Well, in my world it really does. On a professional level I'm passionate about enterprise architecture, about doing the right thing in the right way.

In 2011, at the age of 37, I returned to full time studying at the University of Leuven to pursue formal education in embedded systems. This hardware world became a very important counter-part to my interest in software.

Beyond computers, my family and personal interests complete the picture of my world. This website contains information at various levels—mostly notes, sometimes in-depth introductions.
